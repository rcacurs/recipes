Ingredients:
 - 2 Eggs
 - 4 Slices of bread
 - salt
Directions:
 1. Prepeare pot with water and put eggs in the water.
 2. Heat pot on a cooker until water starts to boil.
 3. Kepp eggs boiling for 10 minutes.
 4. Drain hot water from pot and fill it with cold water to cool the eggs. 
 5. Take 4 slices of bread and top them with butter.
 6. Slice eggs and put slices on the brea.
 7. Pour salt over eggs.
